    KINGDOM KLOTHED FOUNDATION
    THE GREATER VISION

    Now, let me introduce you to the vision of Kingdom Klothed Foundation which is ONE of the pillars of this designer label. 

    Kingdom Klothed Foundation is a non-profit organization aiming to train women locally and in less privileged countries in the skill of craft, embroidery and sewing. Once their skill level improves to a point where they can create special packaging and other items such as accessories, these items will form part of the online store, and all profits will go to these women and their families. We also rely on any donations and sewing tools or supplies for our project. We are hereby empowering their communities and starting the process of creating a fair and equal workforce for Kingdom Klothed. Kingdom Klothed Foundation is yet to be registered and established in the very near future. You who are the buyer, you who are the owner, you are making the impact of the greater vision. From the start, 10% of the profits of each purchase goes to building this foundation. 

    My husband and I work in the ministry as pastors with a heart for missions and this is the beautiful part where it all comes together. By experience many times the work of God gets delayed because of the lack of income on the mission field. Also the majority of less privileged pastors' wives do not have many skills to contribute to their household income. By training them up and making them part of Kingdom Klothed Foundation they will receive an income that will derive from producing items for Kingdom Klothed and will serve as the sustaining factor for them and their churches.

    Once the foundation has grown, Kingdom Klothed will at a later stage then start producing designs that are not limited, but rather limited for a season. These items will also fall in a ready-to-wear category and be produced by these very same women who got enabled because of you.

    For us, Kingdom Klothed is not just a job or business, but a pursuit to equip and change lives.

    We thank each one of you who is part of this vision. We value your support, your prayers and love.

    God bless you
