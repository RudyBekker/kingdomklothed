    This jacket is representative of Kingdom Klothed as a whole. It speaks about the Kingdom of God and the manifestation thereof here on earth. This tailored jacket is constructed from genuine black leather, so you can be rest assured it will last a while, even longer;) It's fun and sexy and communicates boldness and authority.

    The statement golden Embroidery on the back "Your Kingdom Come" in fancy font, will leave people in awe and wonder of curiosity. I'd like to think of it as a conversation starter ;) This leather jacket is not cold on your skin when you put it on, but rather more enjoyable than the cold leather feel, it is warm inside because it is lined with a thin polyester fleece material.

    The inside is real special, every owner of this jacket will come to know the mandate of the 7 mountains in the Kingdom of God and live out their part on their mountain. The blue sky and 7 icy mountains along with Matthew 6:10 is sublimated onto the lining. The trimmings are not like a shiny gold but rather vintage looking gold zippers are used to compliment the black leather. The front zipper is stitched diagonally to create the overlay collar look. The side "accessory" zippers are not just there to look fancy, those zippers actually have pockets ;)

    Your jacket is custom made to your size and also personalized to you by engraving your name along with the garment name and your collection number onto the gold plated aluminium label. There are only 12 of these jackets in the whole world and serve as an exclusive collectors item.

    May every owner testify of a growing boldness that they experience, because of proclaiming it, praying it, believing it and speaking it over themselves. May God's Kingdom come down and come into your life like never before!
