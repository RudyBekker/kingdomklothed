    This Merino wool sweater is so dear and close to my heart.

    The cut is loose fitting, comfortable and snug. With the fold over turtle neck you won't need a scarf to keep you warm and the narrowing cuffs will keep out the cold. The bodice is not too short, but hangs comfortably just over the top half of your backside. The bodice does taper a bit inward but not too much that it sits tight, it still hangs comfortably.

    The colors and what it represents are what makes this sweater so significant. The beige color on the right arm side represents Jesus's body which is the bread that we break when taking communion so that we may receive healing because of His body that broke for us. The scarlet to fading red color on the left arm side represents Jesus's blood that flowed from the cross so that all our sins could be forgiven and washed white as snow.

    The reason for choosing Merino wool is because of its smart properties. It reacts to changes in your body temperature. So it helps you stay warm when the weather is cold and cool when the weather gets warmer. It absorbs moisture and odor. With the very fine fibers, it feels soft and gentle against your skin.

    There will only be 4 owners of the Communion sweater in the whole world. Your sweater is personalized with an embossed leather label containing your name, the garment name and the collection number. This one is very exclusive and serve as a collector's item.

    This sweater is very sacred and special. It represents something so beautiful and pure our minds cannot even fathom what happened there. Something happens in the spirit when we take communion. We must take it as often as possible. Jesus' body broke for our healing and His blood flowed for our sins. We are to be ever thankful for His never ending grace and love.
